Horizon Christian School just recently applied for the Kroger Community Rewards Program! Through this program, we can very easily earn money for our school. The cool thing about this program is that, whenever you shop at Kroger and use your Kroger Plus Card, Horizon Christian School will now earn rewards! It’s so easy – after someone in your household enrolls his or her card, all linked Kroger Plus Cards will also begin earning funds! In order for our organization to earn this money though, you must enroll your Kroger Plus Card. Steps listed below:

Do you use your phone number at the register? Call 800-576-4377, select option 4 to get your Kroger Plus card number.

Members must swipe their registered Kroger Plus card or use the phone number that is related to their registered Kroger Plus card when shopping for each purchase to count.
